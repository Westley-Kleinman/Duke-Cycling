Submit a Ride

Propose a club ride for the community. Your submission will be reviewed and added to our calendar.

Submission Guidelines

  • • Submit rides at least 24 hours in advance
  • • Include clear meeting location and time
  • • Specify appropriate pace for the intended group
  • • Provide route details when possible
  • • All riders must follow Duke Club Cycling safety guidelines
